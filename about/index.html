About The Site

Spoon And Birdcage was built from the ground up to be fast, responsive and easy to maintain. With that in-mind I decided to go with Jekyll to compile a static site, Bourbon and Neat for a super lightweight framework, grunt for a task runner and the whole thing is being hosted through GitHub.
